We met online on December 28, 2024, and from our very first conversation, there was an instant connection. Our first date was on July 29, 2025, on my birthday, making it extra special. We continued to see each other with meetings on August 1, August 6, and August 12.

On September 5, 2025, You let me say "I love you" for the first time, and on September 30, we shared our first hug a moment we'll both cherish forever.

Like every relationship, we've had our share of challenges and fights, but we've always managed to work through them together. For us, overcoming these obstacles has become our greatest strength and has made our bond even stronger.

This website is our digital scrapbook a place to record and cherish our journey together as we continue to build our little world.
